GUILD-BOT Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

GUILD-BOT stores your Discord User ID only for the purpose of tracking whether you have already seen the initial notice.
This ensures you don't repeatedly see the same prompt and improves your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your Discord ID is used exclusively for managing the first-time notice system.
We do not collect, log, or process any other personal information.

3. Data Sharing

GUILD-BOT does not sell, share, or disclose your information to any third parties.

4. Cookies and Tracking

The Bot does not use cookies, analytics tools, or other tracking technologies.

5. Data Deletion and Access

Because we only keep your Discord ID temporarily for user notice functionality, and no other personal data is stored,
there is no need for data deletion or export requests.
If you have concerns, you may still reach out to us.

6. Paid Services

GUILD-BOT is a free service and does not provide paid features or subscriptions.

During the server onboarding process, GUILD-BOT may briefly scan public message content to detect metadata such as server tags or invite links.
